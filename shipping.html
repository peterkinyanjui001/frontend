How Do We Ship Worldwide?

We are delighted to bring our artwork to collectors around the world, ensuring that each piece arrives in pristine condition. Our shipping process is designed to protect your artwork while providing you with a seamless experience.

Packaging & Handling



Once your order is confirmed, we take great care in preparing your artwork for shipment. Each piece is:

  • ✅ Wrapped in acid-free paper to protect its surface.
  • ✅ Cushioned securely with high-quality bubble wrap for added protection.
  • ✅ Depending on the format, either rolled in a sturdy shipping tube or placed in a reinforced box to prevent damage.

We ensure that your painting is carefully handled and securely packaged to withstand transit.

Shipping Partners & Tracking



We ship worldwide using trusted courier services, ensuring fast and reliable delivery. Once your package is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email, allowing you to monitor its journey in real time.

Shipping Costs & Timeframe

The cost of shipping depends on several factors, including:

  • The size and weight of the artwork
  • The destination country
  • The selected shipping method (standard or express delivery)

After your order is placed, we will carefully measure and weigh the artwork to provide you with an accurate shipping quote. You will be informed of the final shipping cost before payment is processed.

Estimated delivery times vary depending on location:

  • Africa: 3–7 business days
  • Europe & North America: 5–10 business days
  • Asia & Australia: 7–14 business days

For any special shipping requests or urgent orders, please contact us before making a purchase, and we will be happy to assist.

Customs & Duties

International shipments may be subject to customs duties or import taxes imposed by the destination country. These charges are the responsibility of the buyer and may vary depending on local regulations.

Returns & Refunds

We take great care to ensure that each painting reaches you in perfect condition. However, if your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 7 days with photos of the damage. We will arrange for a replacement or refund where applicable.

Care Instructions

To maintain the quality and longevity of your painting:

  • Avoid direct sunlight to prevent fading.
  • Keep artwork away from moisture and extreme temperatures.
  • Frame your painting for added protection and preservation.

Shipping Insurance

We offer optional shipping insurance to cover any loss or damage during transit. If you would like to add insurance to your order, please let us know before checkout.
